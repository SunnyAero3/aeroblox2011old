ROBLOX Ambassador Program

Join the ROBLOX Ambassador Program and help us get the word out about ROBLOX! Earn tickets and help other gamers like yourself find ROBLOX! A bigger community means more players visiting places, more people to play against, and more people creating new and original levels.

Here are the two big things to know about the Ambassador Program:

1. We award 2 tickets per day for each link. There is currently an award cap at 50 live links.

2. We encourage you to post links on personal home pages, blogs, and profiles, but please don’t post to forums. We don’t want to overwhelm other sites, and we will not credit links to sites that could get overloaded.

Create Your Link

Post the following link text on the target page. Remember – personal home pages, blogs, and profiles.


HTML Text Link

Please provide the exact URL where your link will be posted.

example: http://www.samplewebsite/sampledirectory/samplepage.html

Pfft. ROBLOX.com doesn't count.


Notes

After submitting your link, we will check it. Once we validate it, your link will have a green icon next to it. Please check out Builderman's Help Page for what all the different status icons mean.

You can only get credit for one link per domain per ROBLOX user. For example, you can't get credit for two links whose url starts with www.samplewebsite.com

Please note that we can only find links on pages available to the general public. For example, if you post your link to your Facebook profile, we will not be able to find it, because a public internet user cannot browse your Facebook profile. You are still welcome to post links in private places, but you will not be able to get credit for them.
